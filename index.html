Why Rangeway

Charging is a hospitality problem. We're the hospitality solution.

The EV charging industry has treated charging as a utility problem. But drivers spend 20 to 40 minutes at a charging location. That's not a transaction. It's an experience. And right now, that experience is broken.

Rangeway is building something different: a premium charging network where every stop is designed around you, not just your vehicle. We think like hotel operators, not utility companies.

73% of EV drivers rate current charging experiences as "poor" or "very poor" J.D. Power, 2025
67% cite lack of amenities as a top complaint
54% cite no weather protection at charging locations

Our Founder

Hospitality DNA Meets EV Passion

Zak Winnick, Founder and CEO, brings over 15 years of experience across hospitality operations and technology. His professional background includes IT leadership and hospitality operations at Wynn Las Vegas, Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas, Grand Hyatt Nashville, and Sensei wellness retreats.

As Community Director of the Bay Area Rivian Club and co-founder of NorCal EVs, Zak combines deep hospitality expertise with firsthand EV community insight. That combination drives Rangeway's mission to transform charging from a logistics burden into a premium experience.

"The EV charging industry has treated charging as a utility problem when it is actually a hospitality problem. Drivers deserve better, and we're building it."
